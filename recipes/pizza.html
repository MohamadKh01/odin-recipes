Pizza

Short on time? Try this tortilla pizza recipe. It comes together quickly and easily with just a few cheap ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings, sprinkle it with cheese
  3. bake again until the cheese is melted
